Why Incognosco?

Incognosco LMS is an innovative e-learning platform tailored for educational institutions. Our dynamic software empowers students and teachers with an online environment to create, manage, and consolidate course materials. We foster a collaborative atmosphere that nurtures individual strengths, encourages academic integrity, and cultivates lifelong learning.

Mission

● Provide engaging learning opportunities and fostering enthusiasm for instruction.
● Identify and nurture the unique strengths of each individual, helping them excel and develop their abilities.
● Cultivate a culture of academic integrity and inspiring passionate students.
● Promote lifelong learning in a collaborative environment.

Vision

Together we can succeed in learning what tomorrow brings ahead of us.


Our Services

Course Enrollment

Incognosco enables students to enroll in appropriate courses with an access code issued by their instructor or educator.

Progress Tracking & Performance

Incognosco lets its users track and monitor their academic progress timely.

Account Creation

Students and instructors may design their profile and enroll in different courses using an access code.

Web-Conferencing Tool

Students and instructors may conduct synchronous classes.

Live Chat

Users have the ability to message and engage in communication regarding pertinent topics, facilitating contact and interaction with others for inquiries and discussions.

Course Content Creation

Incognosco empowers students and instructors to create diverse course materials while ensuring each course is well-organized and structured, enabling seamless navigation and interaction with the content.

Push Notification

Incognosco offers a customizable notification feature that keeps users informed with relevant updates, reminders, and information, giving users full control over their notification preferences within their accounts.

Create Assessments & Quizzes

Instructors can generate and assess quizzes and assessments, automating the grading process and making evaluation more efficient.

Personalized Interface

Users may choose what their interface would look like if they want it to be in light or dark mode.

Incognosco: Collaborative Education FAQ's


How do I log into Incognosco as a student?

To sign in with your existing account, open the Schoology app in your web browser or mobile device:

1. Go to app.incognosco.com.

2. Enter your email address and password and tap Sign In to complete.


Can Incognosco support different types of content?

Yes, Incognosco: Collaborative Education can support various types of content, including text documents, presentations, videos, audio files, images, and interactive quizzes. This flexibility allows organizations to deliver diverse and engaging learning experiences.


Can Incognosco support collaborative learning and discussion forums?

Yes, like any other LMS, Incognosco include features to facilitate collaboration and interaction among learners. These features include discussion forums, chat functionalities, social learning tools, and group projects, fostering knowledge sharing and peer-to-peer engagement.


How does Incognosco manage support and training for administrators?

Incognosco: Collaborative Education Co. offers support and training materials to educational institutions' administrators who are in charge of managing their system. These resources encompass documentation, knowledge bases, tutorials, and responsive customer support channels. They aim to assist with troubleshooting, implementation, and system administration tasks.


Is my personal data secure on Incognosco?

Incognosco takes data security seriously. We use advanced encryption and follow industry best practices to safeguard your personal information and ensure your privacy.
